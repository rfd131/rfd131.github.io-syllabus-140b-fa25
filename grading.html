Earning Experience Points

There are approximately 870 XP available throughout the semester. You earn XP through:

Activity XP Earned Details
LA Community Learning Sessions 20 XP Per week attended
Pre-lecture Assignments (PLAs) 5 XP 80% correct before class (multiple attempts allowed)
Lower scores are scaled
Online Homework (Weekly) 10 XP Percentage of available points (multiple attempts allowed)
Example: 50% of available points earns 5 XP
Target Practice 10 XP Practice problems for each Learning Target. Percentage of available points.
Weekly Surveys 2 XP Brief weekly reflection survey and quiz sign up

Note: Course engagement at a satisfactory level typically corresponds to earning about 500-600 XP (enough for 2 modifications).
