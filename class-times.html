Tuesday Recitations - Learning Target Quiz Sessions

Important: Tuesday sessions are REQUIRED and are dedicated to Learning Target Quizzes. You must register for a specific Tuesday recitation section that may have a different time and instructor than your MWF section.

Each Tuesday session is a 50-minute assessment period where you will take Learning Target Quizzes. These are low-stakes assessments with multiple opportunities to demonstrate proficiency throughout the semester.

LA Community Learning Sessions

Evening Support Sessions

Our Learning Assistants (LAs) and Teaching Assistants (TAs) hold evening office hours to provide peer support and collaborative learning opportunities.

Schedule:
  • Tuesday evenings: 6:00 - 8:00 PM
  • Wednesday evenings: 6:00 - 8:00 PM
  • Thursday evenings: 6:00 - 8:00 PM
What to expect:
  • Help with homework problems and concepts
  • Practice problems for upcoming Learning Target Quizzes
  • Study groups and peer collaboration
  • Review of lecture material

Locations for LA Community Learning Sessions will be posted on Canvas. Attending these sessions earns 10 XP per week!
