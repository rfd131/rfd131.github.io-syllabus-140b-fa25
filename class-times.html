Tuesday Recitations - Learning Target Quiz Sessions

Important: Tuesday sessions are REQUIRED and are dedicated to Learning Target Quizzes. You must register for a specific Tuesday recitation section (labeled 12R-18R) that may have a different time and instructor than your MWF section.

Each Tuesday session is a 50-minute assessment period where you will take Learning Target Quizzes. These are low-stakes assessments with multiple opportunities to demonstrate proficiency throughout the semester.

LA Community Learning Sessions

LA Sessions

Learning Assistants run community learning sessions most weeks during the semester. These are structured problem solving sessions where students work in groups reviewing recently covered class material.

What to expect during LA Community Learning Sessions:

Locations for LA Community Learning Sessions will be posted on Canvas. Attending these sessions earns 20 XP per week!
